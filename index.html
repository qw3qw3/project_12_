A data problem

Cultural minorities can’t find the culturally competent care they deserve, even though 50% of Americans will be a member of at least one underserved community by 2045.

A human solution

We’ll publicly recognize the culturally competent clinicians within your existing provider network(s) so your members can find the right provider. Culturally competent care results in better patient engagement, experiences, and clinical outcomes.

Cultural competence matters.

How it works

Benchmarking

We aggregate and analyze important data about each provider's identities, professional experiences, education, and more.

Badging

Our benchmarking yields useful insights, which are turned into cultural competency badges for the providers that are best suited to work with each community (e.g. BIPOC Competency, LGBQ Competency, etc.). These badges allow your members to find the right provider for them.

Acquisition & growth

In addition to our badges connecting patients to the right care for them, our platform allows provider organizations to organically reach patients and improve quality of care.

A platform made with clinicians at top of mind.

Our platform connects minority patients to the right doctors, allowing provider organizations to grow and payers to reduce cost.
